About

I am a Front End Developer and Visual Artist. My aim is to join these skillsets together, and create projects, that are just as functional as visually pleasing.
I'm excited about exploring the possibilities in web development, and getting familiar with more technologies. I'm always curious about new ideas and opportunities, and passionate about learning!

Currently London based.
