About

I am a Front End Developer and Visual Artist. I aspire to follow my passion and become a UI designer. My aim is to join my different skillsets together, and create beautiful experiences for the web.
I'm excited about exploring the possibilities in UI/UX design, and getting familiar with more technologies. I'm always curious about new ideas and opportunities, and passionate about learning!

Currently London based.
